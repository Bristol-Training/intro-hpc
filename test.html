Pau Erola, PhD

Research Software Engineer at the Jean Golding Institute, University of Bristol

About

I am a researcher specializing in cancer epidemiology, bioinformatics, and data science. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Universitat Rovira i Virgili and M.Sc. in Biomedicine from Universitat de Barcelona, both in 2016.

Currently, I work as a Research Fellow at the Jean Golding Institute, University of Bristol, and hold an adjunct professorship at Universitat Oberta de Catalunya. My research focuses on developing innovative approaches to cancer research through computational methods and data-driven insights.
